Covid-19 Self Assesment Test

If you were exposed to COVID-19 (new coronavirus), have symptoms, or want to be screened, take this self-assessment.

You will be given advice on what to do

You can even take it on someone else's behalf.

The self-assessment is just for informational purposes and cannot be used to diagnose you. Anyone who is unwell or has any disease symptoms, even those not specified in this screening tool, should stay at home and seek medical attention if necessary. If you have medical concerns, seek advice from a physician.
